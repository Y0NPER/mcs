Frequently Asked Questions

What grades do you offer?

+

We offer complete education from Kindergarten (KG) through Grade 12 at both our Addis Ababa and Mekele branches.

How can I enroll my child?

+

You can contact us via phone at 011 663 6605 or visit either of our branches. Our admissions team will guide you through the enrollment process.

What is your student-teacher ratio?

+

We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention for each student, with an average ratio that allows for effective learning.

Do you offer extracurricular activities?

+

Yes! We offer various extracurricular activities including sports, arts, music, drama, science clubs, and more to support holistic development.

Can I schedule a campus visit?

+

Absolutely! We encourage parents to visit our campuses. Please call us at 011 663 6605 to schedule a tour.
