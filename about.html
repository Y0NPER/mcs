About Magic Carpet School

Excellence in Education Since Our Founding

Our Story

Magic Carpet School was founded with a vision to transform education in Ethiopia. What started as a small institution has grown into a respected educational establishment with two thriving branches serving communities in Addis Ababa and Mekele.

Our name "Magic Carpet" symbolizes our commitment to taking students on an educational journey that opens doors to endless possibilities and opportunities.
