Welcome to MCS

Magic Carpet School (MCS) is a leading educational institution in Ethiopia, dedicated to providing world-class education from Kindergarten through Grade 12. With branches in Addis Ababa and Mekele, we serve hundreds of students with excellence and dedication.

Our commitment is to nurture not just academic excellence, but also character, creativity, and critical thinking skills that prepare students for success in an ever-changing world.
